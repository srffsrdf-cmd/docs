The following are some FAQ's about the training division. This page serves to answer them :

  ### "How do I post a training ?"
Use the format to post a training in the [Trainee Request](https://discord.com/channels/1318039349220081756/1514102123506171964) Discord Channel. Add a check mark onto your message and wait for 20-30 minutes, then post a secondary message with a ping to any recruit that has added to your check mark.

  ### "How do I apply for the Training Division ?"
The application for the Training Division is posted in the channel **(INSET CHANNEL LINK)**, you may apply through filling in the applications. Applications are reviewed every week on Friday.

  ### "How Long Are Trainings ?"
Training length depends on different factors, for example, how many people are attending the training, how many trainers there are. On average a training will last between 30-45 minuntes with the average amount of recruits all working diligantly and professionally. 

  ### "Do I Need To Research Anything Before The Training ?"
No, every and all procidures for trainings are given in the following documentation. If you have any questions during your training, feel free to ping someone of a high Training Rank than you and they will be happy to answer.

Any questions gained though trainings or out of trainings are happy to be answered within the channels. All questions should be answered within a 24h time period, ensure you do not ping someone who is either on LOA or show to be unavalible, also ensure that you nly ping someone once per 12-24h.
