The following are some FAQ's about the training division. This page serves to answer them :

  ### "How Do I Get Trained ?"
Requestion A Training in the [Trainee Request](https://discord.com/channels/1318039349220081756/1514102123506171964) Discord Channel. Make sure to follow the template.

  ### "What Do I Do After I Get Trained ?"
After a training you can, go on shift, look into divisions within the department or just nothing. It's advised that you go on shift as soon as possible to get promoted but it is up to the recruit.

  ### "How Long Are Trainings ?"
Training length depends on different factors, for example, how many people are attending the training, how many trainers there are. On average a training will last between 30-45 minuntes with the average amount of recruits all working diligantly and professionally. 

  ### "Do I Need To Research Anything Before The Training ?"
Simple answer, No. All information required to be a Trooper is given out in the training itself. However, previous experiance and knowledge on operations, police codes, etc can give you an edge against your other recruits.

In and out of trainings, trainers will always be willing to answer any questions not covered here. All trainings have multiple timeslots for answering any questions recruits may have, these timeslots may also be used for breaks etc.
