### Directing the Recruit
When a recruit reacts to your message, you are to tell them in [this Discord channel](https://discord.com/channels/1318039349220081756/1514102560347259020) to join the ER:LC private server. The code you need is at the top of this message. 

Once they join, follow these instructions:
1. Tell them to join the **Whitelisted Team**.
2. Once they have joined the **Sheriff Team**, you can proceed to tell them to put their uniform on.
3. They can wear any uniform level (**Class A**, **Class B**, or **Class C**).
4. Make sure you put on your **Trainer uniform**.
