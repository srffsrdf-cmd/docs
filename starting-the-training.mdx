### Posting a Training
Afer fully becoming a member of the Training Division, you will be advised to host a training. Hosting a training is as follows, after reacting to a training request you are permitted one message underneth with the following phrase:

` Recruit [Recruits Ping], please join the training server ( **Code TALFHP** ) as well as the **Training VC**. If you do not join within **five minutes** of the time you have allocated, then your training will **canceled** and you will have to be trained at another time. ` 

After placing these instructions, you are permitted to join the training server.

### Recruit Attendence 
After joining the training server, you are pemitted to stay in it until the dedicated time of the recruits training. If the recruit attends and the approriate time then you are to take a role call based on who shows up and at what time.

If the recruit is late up to 5 minutes then you are to record the amount of minutes they are late and log it when taking role. 

If the recruit shows up after 5 minutes or does not show up at all then you are to record there failure to show up to training. Only then are you free to go about your day.

### In-game Directions
After all trainers and recruits are in-game, you will need to direct the recruit. The instructions should be placed as follows : 

1. Tell them to join the **Whitelisted Team**, (*This should be the sherrifs team*).
2. Once they have joined the **Sheriff Team**, you can proceed to tell them to put their uniform on.
3. They can wear any uniform level (**Class A**, **Class B**, or **Class C**).
4. Make sure you put on your **Trainer uniform**.

After this, ensure you and the recruit/s go to the Sherrif briefing room. In the event another training is happening, you are permitted one of two things you can

1. Go to the PD Briefing Room
2. Join in the training and help the other training. (*This will only apply if the trainer is just starting, do not join a training if it is any amount into the content.*)
